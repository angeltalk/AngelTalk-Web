Application for children  with Angelman Syndrome

AngelTalk is built to help the children who have special needs communicate with their parents and caregivers pleasantly and quickly.

It is a mobile app suitable for those who just decided to start using Augmentative and Alternative Communication Tools.

KEY FEATURES

CARDS WITH REAL IMAGE & VOICE

Use real pictures or short video clips of the things that your child want to express. Record your voice to gain your child’s attention.

CARDS ON LOCK SCREEN

Worrying about abuse of smartphone by your child? Using the lock screen of your smartphone, You can hand it to your child, anytime you want.

MANAGE CARDS EASILY

Wanna hide some cards that are not appropriate at this moment, or change some cards? It takes only a few seconds to do all things above.

SHARE CARDS INSTANTLY

Share cards to family members, school teachers, and language pathologists. You can share instantly via text messages or KakaoTalk.

HOW TO USE ANGELTALK

It is simple, easy and pleasant to use. If you follow the instructions below, You won’t wonder around the screens.

  • MAKE A CARD
  • USING LOCKSCREEN
  • MANAGE CARDS
  • SHARE CARDS

Make your own card with Real Image and Voice

                        Use real pictures or short video clips of the things that your child want to express, and record your own voice to gain your child’s attention.


                        i. Prepare something you want to take a picture of it.

                        ii. Before running the app,  choose the name of the card carefully and practice to pronounce it correctly, so that your child can easily recognize your voice and its meaning clearly.

                        iii. Run the app on your smartphone and choose one of the existing categories where your card will be placed.

                        iv. Tap the ‘New Card’ button,  and follow the instructions.
                    
                        * In some circumstances, you might not have enough time to make the card completely. 
                        Don’t worry about it. In which case just capture the picture of what you want by using your camera app. 
                        Later, you can make the card with that picture.

Show Cards on Lock Screen of your smartphone

                        Worrying about abuse of smartphone by your child?
                        Our app uses the lock screen of your smartphone. You can hand it to your child, anytime you want.


                        i. Pull down the notification drawer of your smartphone.

                        ii. Find the AngelTalk among a bunch of bars, and press the ‘ON’ button in the AngelTalk bar.

                        iii. Now, AngelTalk app will appear on the lock screen. You can hand your smartphone to your child.
                    
                        * If you are not with your child, then press the ‘OFF’ button in the notification bar, it won’t appear on the lock screen.

Manage Cards Easily

                        SHOW/HIDE 
                        At a particular time, you may think that some cards are not appropriate to show them to your child.
                         Instead of deleting those cards permanently, you can just hide them so that your child are not able to see them in AngelTalk.


                        CHANGE SORT ORDER 
                        As your child grows, his or her interest changes. You can place the most attractive card in upper position and lower the position of less intersting cards. Your child doesn’t need to swipe too many cards to find a proper one.


                        CHANGE CARD NAME, VOICE, etc.
                        It’s easy and simple. It takes only a few second to change those.
